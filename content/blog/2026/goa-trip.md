## 24th April

With Pratham and Anurag sir, we moved around 09:15 from Pune guest house. We took the 100km extra long route to see the
scenery of **Mulshi Lake**. We had Sandwich and Cold Coffee at a restaurant nearby. We reached our rented flat at ~9
o'clock. We checked-in and immediately walked to **Fish Deck** a nearby restaurant for dinner. After that, we decided to
see the beach which is around 1.5km away. Just after 400m-500m of walking we felt the humidity and sweating it was
disorienting of will to go there but eventually reached and enjoyed the scenery of **Anjuna Beach**. I called my college
friends in Bhopal who doesn't know that I was on a trip to Goa. That is intentional because I wanted to surprise them
and flex before them on the group video call. After sitting for some time on the beach, we returned to our flat to bath
and slept.

## 25th April

Next day, I woke at 12 noon. Anurag sir has gone for a haircut and we go to **Anjuna Flea Market** to buy some clothes
which is suitable for this humid weather. We haven't ate that day until 17 but eventually found a Punjabi restaurant
where I ate Chicken Biryani and Lassi, it was fabulous. Me and Anurag sir ordered the same Hydrabadi Chicken Biryani
each, when the order arrived it was huge we should have ordered only one for both of us. Even then I ate all of my part
but Anurag sir left some of it. Pratham sir is vegetarian so he ate Paneer, Roti and Lassi. Then we go to **Baga Beach**
which is full of crowd. We stayed there about 10-15 minutes and moved to pick up Aditya sir from **Dabolim Airport**.
Finally, we shake hands. With Aditya sir onboard we reached our flat to get ready for the **Savara Night Club** opening.
Manik also came to the flat to pick us.

It's my first time at a club. I checked one point in my experiences list after experiencing that I had written some
words for that:

> I think, it increased the hearing and lower my thoughts handling capabilities. I am feeling a little dizzy not too
> much, its just I am moving faster. It's like everything seems faster now. Surprisingly, I feel confident and I am not
> able to concentrate on my thought for a thought I just do whatever comes first. I gone to washroom and choose the very
> first seat and this is seriously not me. This feeling faded away after sometime _(I was keeping track of time)_.
>
> While having it I was just thinking one thing, why someone wants this bitter taste constantly to feel unconscious,
> there are better alternative.

Around 3AM we reached flat and slept.

## 26th April

We had lunch at **Goan Spices** and came to **Anjuna Beach** to enjoy the beach and sea. Only Me, Pratham and Aditya sir
bath in the sea, Anurag sir stayed at shore and guard our clothes and phone. We didn't rest in the sea. We stopped the
waves so that nobody behind us will be able to play with it. After some time I found a ball floating in the sea and then
we started catching and diving with the ball. Another group of boys also joined us in the play. We played it to the
fullest. Then we had dinner at **Fish Deck**.

## 27th April

We had a meeting, so we gone to **Coffee Central** cafe for that. After that we were wandering around in Flea Market and
around 21 we left.
