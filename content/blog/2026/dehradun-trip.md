Its again only us, Anshul, Ankit and Tanu Didi 6 months after Jaipur trip. Dedicating our next 4-5 days to Dehradun
Trip.

There's so much happened in between these 6 months.

- I joined my college, made several awesome and fantastic friends.
- Stuck my life between college practicals and assignments.
- Traveled 3 times to home without reservation in train (it should also be called as an experience).
- Took one of the important and hardest decision to drop out of college, so that I can go solo.

## I Shouldn't Go

Trip planning is the responsibility of Ankit and Didi, I just review it and give feedback on it. While the trip is at
its planning stage I was indulged in the process of TC and Ankit is in his semester exams.

I was having a hard time on how to get along in life now and there is no case to go on a trip. I want to reflect upon
myself alone while Ankit will be on the trip. But it didn't happen because I am not able to communicate my thoughts.

Also I have decided to go to next trip with my sister and that is not happening.

## In the Trip

Almost 2 hours before the train to NZM, I was convinced to go on the trip. I packed my couple of clothes and put my
shoes and got ready for the trip because it was the time to not get bored by enjoyment. I forgot the past and lived in
the moment. I sacrificed my past and future to live in the moment.

Again I don't have reservation but I bought one general ticket but when the TT checked it isn't the valid general ticket
as the train was superfast and I've taken Mail/Express ticket. The TT told to get the penalty ticket but when it was my
time he was distracted by another and forgot to charge me. Again, I got lucky. (I know this isn't good but there's no
choice)

## Day 01: Awesome Chhota

From NZM, we are going to Ankit's uncle's place to stay till 21:00 so that we can catch the 23:00 bus to Dehradun from
Anand Nagar ISBT.

I met an awesome, artistic, creative girl named SHNY and her older sister PRNM. I specially want to talk about SHNY. She
looks very similar to one of Didi's friend.

Approx 2 hour after we reached, she brought some paper made stuffs like bag, birthday diary, spray, laptop, and many
things. At first thought she would have made these from YouTube videos but I was totally wrong and it's amazing that a
girl this small can create these crafts on her own just by looking and observing things :salute:.

I was astonished by her Laptop, Birthday Diary, Spary Can and I want to demystify each of them and you will also tend to
appreciate her creativity.

### Birthday Dairy

It consists of four pages each saying a sequence of story.

1. Cover page says "Happy birthday to you".
2. A cutout of cake depicting the celebration.
3. A cutout of flower to bring color and happiness in the life.
4. Last page stating the smile on the face of the birthday girl.

### Laptop

Her sister helped her to create this but the most important and nuanced part of this is creation is built by SHNY. The
keypad of the Laptop where anyone of us would just thought to draw the keys but she is different and a nice observer.
She created a spring mechanics by folding the paper for the keys and this detailing is fantastic. The laptop also has
icons of files, folders, YouTube, Settings, etc.

### Spray Can

Once her cousins were playing with some spray and they are not letting her play with it and that's when she decided to
built it her own. She built it with proper detailing like the Can Cap, Tip, Spray foam.

She is just awesome and I wish she would do or create something even more awesome things.

While leaving, she cried out loud but there's nothing we can do but leave.

## Day 02: New City, New Experience

Its time to explore the city from ground by searching for a good hotel with reasonable price.

I thought we can find it with the help of Google Maps but it is quite difficult without any guidance and wheels. A auto
driver approached to show some 2-4 hotel with reasonable price in around ₹50. We accepted the offer and after 4 hotels
we reached a reasonably good hotel with affordable price.

Hotel: Aryan Castle Homestay, Shimla Bypass, Dehradun

We took the single bedroom at ₹2k for one day stay around 07:30. Gave ₹100 to Saheed Ahmed the auto driver.

After changing and resting in about 2 hours we came out to search the bike rental shop to rent 1 bike for three of us
which is quite risky. We asked Saheed Bhaiya (Auto Driver) and suggested "UK07 Bike Rental Planet" but at that time it
was closed. So we searched for another on Google Maps, it was 2.5km far, took auto at ₹80 but couldn't find a right bike
so we fallback to previous bike rental.

We took "Apache RTR 200cc" for ₹900/day and "Royal Enfield Hunter" for ₹1300/day.

After a nice list of traveling point from Bike Rental wale Bhaiya, we first moved towards FRI college. It is well known
for its Bollywood film shooting place. After reaching there we came to know that it was closed for tourists for almost 6
months. Without wasting much time we moved towards Tapakeshwar Mandir, we just have to face traffic for couple of
minutes. After pray before Lord Shiva and other Gods, it was the first time we touched flowing water on the trip. It was
so cold and mesmerizing. It was a moment. Although we (everyone there) are busy taking pictures and videos but no-one
not even me tool a moment to live the moment or try to thank the calmness of the flowing water.

> I believe, traveling is not capturing the moment instead it is a way to live in the moment by forgetting each and
> every of your thoughts and problems.

It is hard to follow up on this thought because I had already tried it on Jaipur Trip I couldn't hold it even there. So
I just go with the crowd and did what others are doing.

After playing enough with slow stream, we ate a little in nearby Dhaaba and moved towards Robber's Cave (Gucchu Paani).
It also has stream but it was speedy and longer. Also it has small waterfall in the end after 300 meter of walk but we
couldn't bath because we didn't plan it and the water was too cold but we walked till the end, it was awesome.

After enjoying there, we are planning to meet on of Didi's friend who study in Dehradun. But first we stopped by on
roadside to take some cinematic and photos. In the middle of the shot heavy rain and thunder came due to that we stopped
at nearby bus stop shade. Just after 5-7 minutes to standing, a guy on scooty slipped on the road because he was speedy
and couldn't control himself on the slippery road. Its good that he was alone on it and with helmet. I think he got a
small injury in his leg. People at the bus stop bring him in the shade and told him to rest till rain stop but after 5-8
minutes he's gone with his injured leg.

As the rain slowed down, we also moved and reached hotel fully soaked. The rainwater was traveling from head to
underwear, even the shoes got wet and the cold weather was cherry on the cake. We changed clothes and took some rest.
It's 7:30, rain has stopped, we are hungry, and Didi's friend is also up for meetup so we decided to ride again. But
there was a problem I didn't bring spare shoes as my shoes was soaked, so I wore the bathroom slipper given by the
hotel. If you are worried about Ankit and Didi then do not because they brought three pairs each.

Didi's friend was 12KM away from hotel, the weather so cold but it wasn't raining. We eventually reached and we all sat
for dinner. At the dinner table we discussed whether should we ride to Mussoorie because of this rain I was worried so
much and at the same time rain started again outside. After finishing the dinner the rain stopped, so we quickly moved
towards hotel. We are also searching for shops to buy warm clothes for next day. I was feeling even colder after dinner
on the bike with the slippers.

We are going to check out from current hotel and take new in Mussoorie to stay at night there but we have one suitcase
which can be quietly cumbersome in the ride. We decided to drop the suitcase at Didi's friend's hostel although it might
cost us 24KM more but it was better to leave the suitcase behind.

## Day 03: Ride up to Mussoorie

We woke up at 08 AM, everyone is getting ready one-by-one we planned to check out by 10 AM. Before getting fresh I gone
to check upon the bikes, they look good. While returning the owner of the hotel called me asked when did you checked in
yesterday? I didn't know. He checked the register and told you did at 07:34 AM and now it is around 08:10 AM which is
more than 23 hours. I was confused because yesterday receptionist told that we can check out by 11:00 AM. But the owner
explained and accepted his mistake and asked for penalty of ₹300 and then we can check out by 10 AM. I got up in the
room and explained the situation. We thought don't get involved in this, just pay and move.

> We learned a new experience that before check in asked about any penalty and the time of check out, clearly.

We changed the plan to drop the suitcase to Didi's friend's hostel instead we are going to drop it at Bike Rental Shop
which is 800 meters away from our hotel. Now we gear up the Map to Mussoorie on phone and triumph the bikes towards
Mussoorie.

Took multiple stop while climbing and took awesome pictures. Stopped at a Lord Shiva Temple. We also stopped at a cafe
(Green Valley Cafe) on the roadside for Maggie and Momos and a nice look of view which have climb. After 1.5 hours of
ride we reached the top and now we are looking for Hotel. Just where we stopped a woman approached us and suggested a
hotel to look. We thought start from this if we not like will move to other.

We parked the bikes just below the hotel, yes below because you have climb almost 50-60 steps to reach the hotel. They
showed two rooms one has no view and one has very nice view. We like the second room which has view so we asked the
charges and for a second I thought what is it, what have I listened does he (owner Nitish Bhaiya) said only ₹3000. I
thought this will be around ₹5k-₹7k because of the view and facilities it has fridge, hair dryer, gas-stove, geyser, and
it was a big enough room. After bargaining we settled down to ₹2,700.

Hotel: Beachwood, Near Mall Road, Mussoorie

We were so excited about the room and view. It was too exotic. We take some rest, clicked many pictures, made a funny
video. Then we moved for Dalai Hills and Buddha Temple. Although road was in maintainance and slant, but we ride through
it easily. We reached and park both bikes for ₹100 at paid parking facility.

You need to climb more than 200 steps to reach the Buddha Statue. Although we reached but I was not feeling well because
of my ear pain. I didn't enjoy that moment, didn't took any picture of mine. After stepping down we ate Corn, it was
delicious. Stand by there for heat. Then we moved and reached hotel. Now we are tired, and I am in severe ear pain. I
talked with Dr. Arvind Uncle as insisted by Papa. He told to take pain killer medicine (Zero Dol SP).

At 08:30 PM, we are ready to go to Mall Road of Mussoorie by walk. We asked the path from Nitish Bhaiya (hotel owner).
Didi and Ankit bought blanket for themselves for ₹200 each and I bought my painkiller. After wandering about 30-45
minutes we rollback to the room and slept.

At night around 12:30 AM, the electricity gone down and now we worried about our Bikes because it was parked below the
hotel and we couldn't saw it because of no light. Me and Ankit both are worried and continuously peeking through the
window to see the bikes but couldn't. Although my pain was vanished by then because of the painkiller. Around 02:30 AM
lights came back, I quickly jump from the bed get near to the window to peek the bikes and they are there with no
hindrance. We all got relief and slept.
