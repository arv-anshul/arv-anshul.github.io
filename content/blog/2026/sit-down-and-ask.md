Come sit down beside me and stay a while. Ask a question to yourself, Why were you walking, climbing, or running?

Now think about one of your closest and ask the same to him/her. If both have similar not if same answer then just take
a moment and don't do anything just sit and sit, nothing else. Maybe see your surroundings, people, dogs, sky, or even a
blank wall. Just sit and think nothing, you may find relief.
