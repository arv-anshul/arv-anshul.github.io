These days I am constantly in doubts, not able to think clearly, not able to work on anything. Not able to think
anything. Don't know how my day goes. Just linger around social media _(YouTube, GitHub, Folo)_. Not enjoying anything
music or movie, nothing.

Then I decided to watch the movie **"96"** because related content is coming as recommendation on the YouTube home page.

I think this is a slice-of-life romantic drama film. Although I've never get into these emotion deeply, but I've
scratched it alone a long time ago. I have been suppressing this emotions for about 5-7 years because I want to
understand it first but it is hard in the current surrounding and environment. So I seek these films and shows like 96,
Past Lives, Meiyazhagan, Dead Poet Society, Gulmohar, etc.
